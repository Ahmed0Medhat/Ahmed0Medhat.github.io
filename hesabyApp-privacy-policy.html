Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Hesaby handles your information.

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Hesaby (حسابي كام). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

Information We Collect

Hesaby collects minimal data to provide you with the best experience:

  • Local Data: Bill details, friends list, and expense records are stored locally on your device
  • Optional Cloud Sync: If you enable sync, your data is securely stored in Firebase
  • Google Account: Only used for authentication if you choose to enable cloud sync
  • Device Information: Basic analytics to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the bill splitting functionality
  • To sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • To send you important updates about the app
  • To improve our services based on usage patterns
  • To display relevant advertisements (for free users)

Advertising

Hesaby uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app
  • Delete: You can delete your data at any time from settings
  • Export: You can export your data as PDF reports
  • Opt-out: You can disable cloud sync and use the app offline only

Children's Privacy

Hesaby is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
